Our Reward System

At Lost Pets, we're not just passionate about pets; we're committed to fostering a community where every lost pet finds its way back home. With that vision in mind, we've crafted an innovative incentive program that transforms every act of kindness into tangible rewards.

Imagine this: you're strolling through the neighborhood when you spot a lost furry friend, looking forlorn and confused. Without hesitation, you spring into action, reporting the sighting through our dedicated platform. Little do you know, that single act sets off a chain reaction of joy and gratitude.

How it works!

1) Earn as you Reunite

For every successful reunion facilitated through your report, you earn reward points. Whether it's a lost kitten nestled in a backyard or a wandering pup on the streets, each reunion earns you points that add up to valuable rewards.

2) Redeem for Perks and Benefits

These reward points aren't just numbers; they're keys to a treasure trove of perks and benefits. From gift vouchers at pet stores to exclusive discounts on grooming services, the possibilities are as endless as your generosity.

3) The Joy of Giving Back

But perhaps the greatest reward of all is the joy of knowing you've made a difference. Every wagging tail and purring cuddle is a testament to your compassion and commitment to our four-legged friends.

4) Community, United

Together, we form a formidable network of pet lovers, united in our mission to create safer, happier communities for our furry companions. With each reunion, we strengthen the bonds that tie us together, building a world where no pet is left behind.

How to get points?

To be able to recieve the points and rewards all you will need to do is gather proof that you have:

  • Found the a missing pet.
  • Kept the missing pet safe and fed.
  • Made sure that the missing pet that you have found is possibly on the website.
  • Contact us showing all of the evidence that you have found a missing pet

    • How will we make sure you get the points?

    Once you have provided all of the evidence of the missing pet we will then go further along to try to find the missing pets owner. This process is potentially long lasting however, in some cases it might be quicker than others. This is the process we take:

  • Gather all of the information you have provided.
  • Gather all of the picture or evidence of the missing pet.
  • Look through the entire database of missing pets in which we have to find the missing pet.
  • Once we have found the missing pet and matched it to its owner. We will then go long to locating and contacting the owner.
  • However, if we cannot find that missing pet in you database then we proceed to contact all of the local missing pet services and all facebook groups for you!
  • But, if there is a overall success with getting the missing pet to the owner we will then add points to your account. Which you will then be able to select the available prizes.

    • So, join us on this rewarding journey. Let's turn every lost pet's tale into a story of hope, resilience, and love. Together, we can make a paw-sitive impact that reverberates far beyond our neighborhoods and into the hearts of pet owners everywhere.
